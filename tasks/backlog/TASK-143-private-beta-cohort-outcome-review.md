# TASK-143: Private Beta Cohort and Outcome Review

**Phase:** Product reset and public beta
**Priority:** critical (P0)
**Status:** backlog
**Area:** product / research / beta operations

## Description

Put Little Imp in the hands of real developers with existing bookmark
collections and determine whether the product produces repeated retrieval value.

## Scope

1. Recruit 5-10 qualified participants using TASK-139 criteria.
2. Observe installation, import, first capture, first search, and first successful
   rediscovery without coaching beyond the documented onboarding path.
3. Collect the agreed privacy-preserving local metrics and structured interview notes.
4. Record extraction failures, capture failures, confusing configuration,
   abandoned flows, and reasons to return or uninstall.
5. Conduct a seven-day follow-up and compare results with stop/continue thresholds.
6. Produce a concise evidence report with direct recommendations and a cut list.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] At least five qualified participants complete onboarding and one retrieval task.
- [ ] Installation, capture, extraction, search, and reuse evidence is recorded consistently.
- [ ] Seven-day follow-up data is available for the cohort.
- [ ] The report makes an explicit continue, narrow, pivot, or stop recommendation.

## Dependencies

- TASK-139 (beta evidence plan).
- TASK-142 (usable beta distribution path).

## Notes

- Do not substitute synthetic fixtures, task reports, or contributor testing for user evidence.
